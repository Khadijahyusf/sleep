Startup Incubation

We organize fixed-term, cohort-based programs, that include mentorship and educational components and culminate in a public pitch event or demo day, where startups get to share their ideas with potential investors.

Startup Acceleration

We help grow startups from an early-stage idea to a company that can stand on their own. Over the years, we have Incubated Startups like Northpad Nigeria, Flingz Network, Trust and Identity Solutions, and many more.

Co-working Space

We provide conducive spaces for a community of creatives from different backgrounds to come to work collaborate and share expertise and explore new ideas. The community is focused on creating a network of geniuses.
